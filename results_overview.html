Eight follow-up analyses test robustness, explain heterogeneity, and turn the region-level outputs into interpretable candidate priorities.

1. Replicate consistency

9 repeated cell-line/model groups had multiple matched Ctrl/JQ1 pairs. U87 and OS-RC-2 were highly consistent; MDA-MB-231, PC3, and COLO320-HSR were variable.

Main Takeaway

BRD4 binding landscapes differ substantially across cancer types, and JQ1 response is not uniform. The added analyses strengthen that conclusion: baseline peak burden does not simply predict response, most vulnerable regions are cancer-type-specific, top candidates can be prioritized around E2F3/TARBP1/FBXL5-like loci, and sensitive contexts carry richer detected regulatory motif programs.
